### Content Types to Create:

#### 1. Comprehensive Guides (Pillar Content)
- **Length**: 2000-3000 words
- **Format**: Step-by-step with screenshots
- **Examples**:
  - "Complete Guide to Filing Consumer Complaints in India 2026"
  - "Amazon Complaint: Complete Process from A to Z"
  - "Consumer Court: Everything You Need to Know"

#### 2. Quick How-To Guides
- **Length**: 800-1200 words
- **Format**: Numbered steps
- **Examples**:
  - "How to File Flipkart Complaint in 5 Minutes"
  - "Jio Complaint Kaise Kare - 3 Easy Steps"
  - "Bank Complaint Letter Format - Copy & Use"

#### 3. Template Pages (Transactional)
- **Length**: 500-800 words
- **Format**: Template + instructions
- **Examples**:
  - "Amazon Refund Complaint Letter - Hindi Template"
  - "Bank Complaint Letter Format - English"
  - "RERA Complaint Format - Download Free"

#### 4. Comparison Articles
- **Length**: 1500-2000 words
- **Format**: Comparison tables
- **Examples**:
  - "Consumer Court vs Consumer Forum: Which is Better?"
  - "NCH vs e-Daakhil: Where to File Complaint?"
  - "Online vs Offline Complaint: Pros and Cons"

#### 5. FAQ Pages
- **Length**: 1000-1500 words
- **Format**: Question-Answer
- **Examples**:
  - "Consumer Complaint FAQs - Top 50 Questions"
  - "E-commerce Complaint FAQs"
  - "Banking Complaint FAQs"

#### 6. News & Updates
- **Length**: 500-800 words
- **Format**: News article

  - "RBI Ombudsman New Process Update"
