## 🎓 Educational Content Guidelines

### Safe Content Examples:

✅ "Here's how to file a complaint" (Process explanation)
✅ "These are your consumer rights" (Education)
✅ "This is a sample template" (Reference)
✅ "Contact official portal for filing" (Direction)
✅ "Consult a lawyer for advice" (Recommendation)

### Avoided Content Examples:

❌ "You should file a complaint" (Legal advice)
❌ "Your case is strong" (Legal opinion)
❌ "Use this exact format" (Legal document)
❌ "We will help you file" (Service offering)
❌ "Guaranteed success" (False promise)
